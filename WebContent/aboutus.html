Our History

The International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement is an international humanitarian movement with approximately 97 million volunteers, members and staff worldwide which was founded to protect human life and health, to ensure respect for all human beings, and to prevent and alleviate human suffering.The movement consists of several distinct organizations that are legally independent from each other, but are united within the movement through common basic principles, objectives, symbols, statutes and governing organisations.

Corporate Leadership

Kaifa Mondal Chief Eathquake Officer

Our CEO, Kaifa, credits his hardworking Bangladeshi immigrant parents who undertook the arduous journey to the shores of Kolkata with the intention of giving their children the best future. His mother's wizardy in the kitchen whipping up the tastiest dishes with whatever is available inexpensively at the supermarket, was his first inspiration to feed the poor children who have faced any kind of natural disaster . He brings his zeal for the good cause to this NGO, pioneering cross-cultural relief connections.

Ankit Singh Chief F Officer

Our CFO, Ankit, as he is affectionately referred to by his colleagues, comes from a long established family tradition in farming and produce. His experiences growing up on a farm in the South 24 Parganas outback gave him the required love for life. As he puts it in his own words, Everything that runs, wins, and everything that stays, pays!

Asif Abdullah Chief Tamasha Officer

Blessed with the most discerning gustatory sense, Asif, our CTO, personally ensures that every affected area should get the help.

Syed Obaiz Ali Executive Officer

Award winning three-star Rescuer with wide International experience having worked closely with whos-who in the disaster Management Team , he specializes in quick rescue and has a great sense in Human Life.
